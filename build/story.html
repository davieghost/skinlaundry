"The 1,500-square-foot clinic, which includes four rooms and is designed to look like a beach house with its open, airy California vibe in New York's Flatiron District."

Revolutionizing the way people clean and care for their skin

We treat clogged, dirty, damaged skin through affordable laser & light treatments delivered in an inviting and comfortable setting, staffed with certified healthcare professionals highly trained on proven SL methods and overseen by a team of medical advisors.

The Skin Laundry Philsophy

We believe healthy skin starts with clean skin... really deep down clean. The type of clean that you can only get by tapping into modern technology and using advanced non-invasive lasers and light therapy to vaporize the dirt and toxins on the surface of the skin and to reach pore deep for completely clean skin. We eliminate the small problems to achieve big improvements.
