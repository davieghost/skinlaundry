FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Skin Laundry's Signature Light & Laser Treatment?

Our Signature Laser & Light Treatment utilizes advanced YAG Laser & technology to completely cleanse your skin by removing all the dirt and bacteria. This helps even out skin tone, decrease pore size, improve scars and blemishes making the skin healthier, stronger and more radiant. The treatment only takes 10 minutes and there is no downtime.

How does it work?

Our unique method involves using a YAG laser and IPL (Intense Pulse Light). The laser actively vaporizes excess dirt, makeup and bacteria from your pores first and foremost; the long term effects of the laser can help with pigmentation and collagen production. The IPL works with the brown and red tones of the skin to even out the skin tone as well as stimulate the natural collagen that lies down in the dermis. The combination of laser and IPL clears tones and conditions the skins. The pores are also minimized so they appear less visible.
